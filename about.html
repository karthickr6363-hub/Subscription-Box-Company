Our Story: Unboxing Happiness

From a small idea to a community of 50,000+ happy subscribers, Pakka! is on a mission to bring joy and discovery to every doorstep.

Our Journey

How Pakka! became the most loved subscription box service

2020

The Beginning

Started in a small apartment with a big dream - to curate amazing products and deliver happiness. Our first box had just 10 subscribers.

2021

Rapid Growth

Expanded to 5,000 subscribers and launched our beauty and tech categories. Moved to our first office and hired our core team.

2022

Innovation & Expansion

Launched AI-powered personalization, reached 25,000 subscribers, and introduced sustainable packaging across all boxes.

2023-2024

Becoming a Leader

Reached 50,000+ subscribers, expanded internationally, and became the #1 rated subscription box service with 4.9/5 stars.

Our Mission

To curate and deliver exceptional subscription boxes that bring joy, discovery, and value to our customers' lives while supporting sustainable and ethical practices.

Our Vision

To become the world's most trusted subscription box service, connecting people with amazing products and creating moments of happiness worldwide.
